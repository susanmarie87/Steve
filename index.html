Stephen F. Biegenzahn believes in flexibility and accessibility. Steve provides exceptional legal representation in all aspects of bankruptcy law. Steve, a graduate of Stanford Law School and has over 40 years of legal experience with 26 years of bankruptcy specialization.

About Stephen

Mr. Biegenzahn is a Court Appointed and Certified Mediator; He is acutely aware of the wisdom of resolving disputes without judicial involvement.
